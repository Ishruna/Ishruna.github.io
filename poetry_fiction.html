Phoenix of Rust and Gold


May 23, 2021
Dedicated to our grandmother who left us on May 23, 2021. I wrote this poem in an attempt to enshrine her essense.

Your Hair, the colour of autumn leaves
Smile, the first monsoon rain
Embrace, a summer veil, tender and tranquil.

Roots, twisted and twined in your veins
Stories in your wrinkles, riveting and mundane.
Blossoms spreading beyond neverland,
Your pensive eyes, true and unforgotten.

Go on, you child of green
Follow the path of the first and serene
Smiles and tears and void and still
We bid you farewell.

Rest in peace, you beautiful rose,
A phoenix of rust and gold,
Rest in peace, Mini.
